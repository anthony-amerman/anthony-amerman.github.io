EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE

What is Emotional Intelligence?

the ability of self-awareness and control of your own emotions/behavior. using that ability to assess situations and make decisions. why am i feeling how im feeling, what can or should i do about it. it also has to do with how well you understand other people, being able to empathise and relate, listen and respond.

How is it different to IQ?

intelligence quotient is a score from intelligence tests that focus on "general" intelligence, abstract reasoning, logic.

Why is it important?

It effects how well you get a long with and understand others, how well you communicate, how well you understand your self. which in turn is important for any interpersonal relationships, jobs, self-care.
